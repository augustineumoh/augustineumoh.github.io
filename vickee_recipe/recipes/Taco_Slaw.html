My Taco Recipe

Description

This is a great cabbage slaw for tacos. The local taco truck serves their chicken tacos with cabbage, cilantro, and lime. This is my attempt to recreate their taco toppings.

Ingredients

  • ½ small head cabbage, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • ½ red onion, minced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, juiced

Step-by-step procedure

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Finely chop the cabbage, carrot, and cilantro. Mince the red onion. Seed and mince the jalapeño.
  3. Combine chopped vegetables and lime juice in a bowl; toss to coat.
